# Expansion and Physical Logistics

## Founding before reclamation

Expansion begins with bounded founding into generated empty systems and later
grows into recovery of broken or abandoned places. Current founding is defined
by [Ships and Expansion](../current/ships-and-expansion.md). Reclamation is not
a current mechanic and its taxonomy remains exploratory.

## Physical expeditions

Outward action is lumpy and physical. Expeditions use ordinary travel physics
and commit people, cargo, time, and Energy rather than invoking an abstract
transfer. Preparedness and committed destination truth determine outcomes;
uncertainty belongs to the player's information state rather than a hidden die
roll at resolution.

Future expeditions may fail and lose population. Player-authored expedition
risk is intentionally distinct from liveness guarantees needed by automated or
delegated logistics. Current bounded founding and anti-strand behavior remain
owned by the current ship contract.

Mission types, roster granularity, stranded convoy states, salvage,
reclamation, and rescue are [exploratory](../ideas/expeditions-and-reclamation.md).
