# Production and Capability Ideas

The committed outcome is vulnerable capability growth, not any particular
resource chain or specialist system. Current recipes and values are owned by
[current design and profile configuration](../current/tuning-profiles.md).

## Multiple production chains

Additional raw resources, intermediate products, refined materials, or branching
recipes could create distinct strategic responsibilities. Before promotion,
define the decision served by each resource, deposits and developments,
recipe-selection rules, deterministic contention, constructive frontier
responsibilities, and short scenarios proving the chain adds strategy rather
than bookkeeping.

## Differentiated development recipes

As the catalog expands, developments could require different combinations of
raw, refined, intermediate, or tertiary goods. Design this with production
chains so every material traces to a concrete responsibility. Define supply
paths, substitutions, commitment accounting, and frontier availability without
copying current profile values into prose.

## Specialists

Specialization could be persistent state on population instead of a specialist
resource counter. Roles might enable surveys, expeditions, reclamation, or
infrastructure while life-support and capacity consequences travel with the
population.

Before promotion, define role-specific decisions, training inputs and duration,
lock or retraining behavior, assignment and travel, upkeep and loss, presentation
without hidden-truth leakage, and deterministic scenarios.

## Pacing and time pressure

Population growth, life-support pressure, slot-to-population ratios, or other
internal clocks could make banking forever unattractive. Any pacing proposal
must expose the source of urgency, avoid hidden solvency screening, and preserve
short deterministic acceptance scenarios.

## Tertiary specialist substrate

Training or sustaining specialists could consume tertiary goods, making
intersystem production necessary for capability growth. This is one candidate
for realizing [capability-over-wealth direction](../direction/margin-and-capability.md#capability-over-wealth),
not a committed chain. Specialist types, tertiary recipes, lock granularity, and
upkeep remain open.
