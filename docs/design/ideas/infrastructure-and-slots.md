# Infrastructure and Slot Ideas

Current mechanics are defined by [Systems and Resources](../current/systems-and-resources.md):
slots are generic, operation is all-or-nothing, and multiple same-body
Extractors draw from one body-owned total in stable slot order.

## Development and territory pressure

Development could become a more explicit multi-tick building loop whose home
capacity eventually reaches legible Energy, resource, or slot diminishing
returns. That pressure could keep develop and expand in tension rather than
letting indefinite home investment dominate. A proposal must define the player
verbs, the visible cap, construction commitment, and deterministic small
scenarios before becoming direction.

## Slot restrictions and bonuses

Bodies or slots could gain development restrictions, throughput modifiers,
Energy modifiers, construction modifiers, storage modifiers, or specialized
suitability. This is worthwhile only when visible differences produce a concrete
strategic choice.

Before promotion, define where compatibility lives, how construction targets are
presented, how generation provides any required compatibility constructively,
and how damaged or reclaimed developments retain modifiers. Do not add
speculative fields to the current schema.

## Partial infrastructure operation

Some developments could consume partial upkeep or recipe inputs and produce
proportionally reduced output rather than operating atomically. A proposal must
identify eligible developments, deterministic bands or rounding, shortage and
priority behavior, and a small scenario showing a meaningful choice without
obscuring physical reconciliation.

## Extractor upgrades and specialization

Extraction might grow through upgrades, tiers, attached facilities, specialized
slots, or logistics investments rather than only another Extractor. A proposal
must define replacement and slot semantics, scaling, deterministic contention,
and why the investment differs strategically from stacking current Extractors.
